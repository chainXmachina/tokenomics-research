# Tokenomics Research

This repository explores tokenomics, economic systems, and incentive design in blockchain networks.

The focus is on understanding how decentralized systems create, distribute, and sustain value through onchain mechanisms.

---

## Focus Areas

- Tokenomics and incentive design
- Economic system modeling
- Onchain behavior and coordination
- Mechanism design in blockchain systems
- Governance and value distribution

---

## Structure

### essays/
Conceptual explorations and short research pieces connecting blockchain systems with economic and philosophical ideas.

### simulations/ (optional later)
Agent-based and system-level simulations of token economies.

### theses/
Summaries and adapted versions of academic work (BSc / BA), translated into a blockchain and tokenomics context.

---

## Approach

This repository is not focused on speculation or market predictions.

Instead, it focuses on:
- understanding underlying mechanisms
- analyzing incentives and system dynamics
- designing more robust economic systems

---

## Goal

The goal is to bridge:
- technical blockchain systems
- economic modeling
- philosophical perspectives on value and coordination
